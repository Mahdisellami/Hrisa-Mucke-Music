## Getting Started

### First Launch

1. **Create an Account**
   - Tap "Register" on the login screen
   - Enter your username, email, and password
   - Optionally add a display name

2. **Connect to Backend**
   - The app connects to `http://192.168.2.155:8000` by default
   - To change: Go to Settings > Connection
   - Enter your backend URL and tap "Test"
   - Tap "Save" if connection successful

3. **Explore**
   - Browse the Home screen for recommendations
   - Search for music in the Search tab
   - Discover new genres in Browse

## Navigating the App

### Sidebar Navigation (Desktop/Tablet)

The sidebar provides quick access to all main sections:

- **Home**: Discovery feed with personalized recommendations
- **Search**: Find tracks, artists, albums, playlists, and users
- **Your Library**: Your playlists, favorites, and recently played
- **Browse**: Explore by genre, mood, trending, and new releases
- **Activity Feed**: See what your friends are listening to
- **Settings**: App configuration and account settings

**Collapse/Expand**: Tap the menu icon at the top

### Mobile Navigation

On mobile, the sidebar is hidden by default:

1. **Open Menu**: Tap the hamburger menu (☰) in the top-left
2. **Navigate**: Tap any menu item
3. **Close Menu**: Tap outside the sidebar or the X button

### Bottom Player

The bottom player bar shows the currently playing track:

- **Song Info**: Tap to open full Now Playing screen
- **Play/Pause**: Tap the center button
- **Next/Previous**: Use skip buttons
- **Progress**: Shows playback progress

## Discovering Music

### Home Screen

Your personalized hub:

**Jump Back In**
- Recently played tracks
- Tap any track to resume

**Made For You**
- **Daily Mix**: Your top picks (30 tracks)
- **Discover Weekly**: Fresh finds (20 tracks)

**Trending Now**
- Popular tracks this week
- Ranked by play count

**Recommended For You**
- Personalized recommendations
- Based on your listening history

### Search

Find anything in your library:

1. **Enter Query**: Type in the search box
2. **Browse Results**: Switch between tabs:
   - All: Combined results
   - Tracks: Song results only
   - Artists: Artist results
   - Albums: Album results
   - Playlists: Playlist results
   - Users: User profiles

3. **Filter**: Some results can be filtered by genre/mood

**Search Tips**:
- Search updates as you type (300ms delay)
- Try artist names, song titles, or album names
- Search is case-insensitive

### Browse

Explore by category:

**Trending This Week**
- Most played tracks in the last 7 days
- Tap "See all" for full list

**Genres**
- Rock, Pop, Hip-Hop, Electronic, Jazz, etc.
- Shows track count for each genre
- Tap a genre to see all tracks

**Moods**
- Happy, Sad, Energetic, Chill, Romantic, etc.
- Perfect for setting the vibe
- Tap a mood to explore

**New Releases**
- Tracks added in the last 30 days
- Stay up-to-date with fresh content

## Managing Your Library

### Your Library Screen

Access your personal collection:

**Quick Links**
- **Favorite Songs**: All your liked tracks
- **Recently Played**: Your listening history
- **Your Profile**: View your public profile

**Playlists**
- Grid view of all your playlists
- Tap "+" to create new playlist
- Tap any playlist to open

**Library Stats**
- Total tracks in your library
- Number of playlists you've created

### Creating Playlists

1. Go to Your Library
2. Tap the "+" button
3. Enter playlist name and description
4. Choose public or private
5. Tap "Create"

### Adding to Playlists

**From Track Menu**:
1. Find a track (Search, Browse, etc.)
2. Tap the three dots (⋯)
3. Select "Add to Playlist"
4. Choose playlist

**From Now Playing**:
1. Open full Now Playing screen
2. Tap the heart icon to favorite
3. Tap list icon to add to playlist

### Managing Playlists

**Edit Playlist**:
- Go to playlist
- Tap "Edit" button
- Update name, description, or privacy
- Tap "Save"

**Delete Playlist**:
- Go to playlist
- Tap "Delete" button
- Confirm deletion

**Reorder Tracks**:
- Tap and hold track
- Drag to new position
- Release to drop

## Social Features

### Following Users

**Find Users**:
1. Go to Search tab
2. Switch to "Users" tab
3. Search by username or display name
4. Tap user to view profile

**Follow**:
- Tap "Follow" button on user's profile
- Their activity will appear in your feed

**Unfollow**:
- Tap "Following" button to unfollow

### Activity Feed

See what your friends are listening to:

**Activity Types**:
- ❤️ Liked a track
- ➕ Created a playlist
- 📝 Added to playlist
- 👥 Followed a user
- 🔗 Shared a playlist
- 🔗 Shared a track

**Interact**:
- Tap an activity to view the track/playlist/profile
- Tap user avatar to view their profile

### User Profiles

View any user's public profile:

**Profile Info**:
- Username, display name, bio
- Avatar (if uploaded)

**Stats**:
- Playlists count
- Followers / Following counts
- Total plays

**Tabs**:
- **Public Playlists**: Playlists they've shared
- **Recent Activity**: Their latest actions

**Your Profile**:
- Access from Library screen
- Tap "Edit Profile" to update bio, display name
- Tap followers/following to see lists

### Sharing

**Share Track**:
1. Open Now Playing screen
2. Tap share button
3. Choose:
   - Share to Activity Feed (friends see it)
   - Share via... (system share sheet)
   - Copy Link

**Share Playlist**:
1. Open playlist
2. Tap share button
3. Makes playlist public
4. Share with friends

## Playback Controls

### Now Playing Screen

Tap the bottom player to open full screen:

**Main Controls**:
- **Play/Pause**: Center button
- **Previous**: Skip to previous track
- **Next**: Skip to next track
- **Shuffle**: Randomize playback order (green = on)
- **Repeat**: Loop current track or all (green = on, "1" = repeat one)

**Progress**:
- Progress bar shows elapsed/remaining time
- Drag to seek

**Volume**:
- Slider at bottom
- Adjust playback volume

**Secondary Actions**:
- **Queue**: View and manage upcoming tracks
- **Share**: Share current track
- **Favorite**: Add to favorites (heart)

**Lyrics** (if available):
- Tap document icon to toggle lyrics view
- Synced lyrics auto-scroll
- Highlighted line shows current position

### Queue Management

View and control upcoming tracks:

1. Tap queue button (list icon)
2. **Now Playing**: Current track
3. **Up Next**: Upcoming tracks
4. **Actions**:
   - Reorder: Drag tracks
   - Remove: Swipe left or tap X
   - Clear: Remove all

### Playback Modes

**Shuffle Mode**:
- Randomizes playback order
- Green icon = active
- Tap to toggle

**Repeat Modes**:
- **Off**: Play through once
- **All**: Loop entire queue
- **One**: Loop current track (shows "1")
- Tap to cycle through modes

## Tips & Tricks

### Keyboard Shortcuts (Web)

- **Space**: Play/Pause
- **←/→**: Previous/Next track
- **↑/↓**: Volume up/down
- **M**: Mute
- **L**: Like current track
- **Q**: Open queue

### Gestures (Mobile)

- **Swipe down** on Now Playing: Close screen
- **Swipe left** on track: Remove from queue
- **Long press** on track: Show context menu
- **Pinch** on album art: Zoom (if applicable)

### Performance Tips

1. **Keep App Updated**: Check for updates regularly
2. **Clear Cache**: Settings > Clear Cache (if implemented)
3. **Stable Connection**: Use WiFi for best experience
4. **Battery Saver**: Lower screen brightness during long sessions

### Customization

**Display Name**:
- Go to Settings > Profile
- Change display name (shown instead of username)
- Add a bio to personalize your profile

**Privacy**:
- Make playlists private (only you can see)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: How do I add music to the app?**
A: Music is managed on the backend. Run `populate_db.py` and `populate_data.py` scripts.

**Q: Can I download music for offline playback?**
A: Not currently implemented. Music streams from backend.

**Q: How do recommendations work?**
A: Based on collaborative filtering (users with similar taste) and your listening history.

**Q: Can I import playlists from Spotify/Apple Music?**
A: Not directly. You can manually add tracks by searching.

**Q: Is there a dark mode?**
A: Yes! The app uses dark mode by default (Spotify-inspired).

**Q: How do I delete my account?**
A: Contact backend administrator or use Settings > Delete Account (if implemented).

**Q: Can I use this on multiple devices?**
A: Yes! Login with same credentials on any device.

**Q: What audio formats are supported?**
A: MP3, FLAC, WAV, M4A (depends on backend configuration).
